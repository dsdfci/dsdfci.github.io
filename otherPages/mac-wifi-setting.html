    • Macintosh Wi-Fi configuration:From the “Apple” menu, click “System Preferences” then “Network“.

    Verify that Wi-Fi is “On” (you may have to click “Turn Wi-Fi On“) and click “Advanced“:

    Next click the “+” to add a preferred network and click “Choose a network“.

    From the list of available Wi-Fi networks, choose phswifi3

    and click “OK“.

    Enter your Partners username and password and click “OK“. At this point, the Airport icon at the top of your screen should be “live”. If you click on it, phswifi3 should have a check mark next to it.

    Please remember that whenever you update your Partners password (as required by Partners), you will need to also update your WiFi settings.
