    DS Mail Setting

    Starting on May 2019 all new users have an Enterprise Google Account, with an email of the form: ‘user’@ds.dfci.harvard.edu

    The Enterprise Google account is HIPAA compliant and includes mail, calendar, and drive. The total user quota for all google applications is 30Gb.

    To access your account, please go to https://account.google.com and sign in as ‘user’@ds.dfci.harvard.edu. You can access all the google applications by clicking the ‘Google apps’ button in the top right corner and selecting the app to use.
